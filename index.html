Driving Innovation and Excellence in Supply Chain and Business

Alliance for Supply Chain Management

About Us

Alliance for Supply Chain Management (ASCM) is an independent society that was founded by a group of supply chain and business enthusiasts, including alumni of Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology, and other universities in Bangladesh. The organization is focused on promoting and developing the potentialities of the next generation of supply chain and management professionals in Bangladesh. ASCM aims to share knowledge and resources regarding supply chain and business among students and young professionals in Bangladesh. By connecting professionals, academicians, and learners, ASCM aims to facilitate business and supply chain insights, as well as technology and managerial thoughts for sustainable growth. The main objective of ASCM is to promote and advance the field of supply chain management in Bangladesh, and to support the development of the next generation of supply chain professionals. The organization offers a range of activities, including seminars, workshops, and networking events, to foster collaboration and learning among members. ASCM is committed to promoting sustainable practices in supply chain management and business, and to creating a forum for the exchange of ideas and best practices in the field. The organization works closely with leading businesses and academic institutions to stay up-to-date on the latest trends and innovations in supply chain management and business.

Mission: To create a platform that connects enthusiasts and professionals to enrich their knowledge and understanding related to business technology, supply chain and management and also how they can apply that knowledge in practice.

Vision: Strive to create professionals with expertise and profound understanding of business technology, supply chain and management who will create a sustainable and positive impact on Bangladesh’s economy through revolutionary change in the supply chain, technology and management of the business sector.

Learning and Development

At ASCM, we ensure that our training and education programs follow global standards in professional learning and development. We believe in providing high-quality learning experiences to our members, and we strive to incorporate the latest industry insights and best practices into our programs. This helps us to equip our members with the skills and knowledge they need to excel in their careers, and to stay up-to-date with the latest developments in supply chain management and business.

Research Activities

ASCM is committed to promoting research and innovation in the field of supply chain management and business. To this end, we organize a range of research and task-based competitions, symposiums, and conferences every year. These events provide our members and beneficiary with opportunities to showcase their skills and knowledge, and to learn from experts in the field. In addition, we organize research training bootcamps, which are designed to provide members with the skills and knowledge they need to conduct research effectively. These boot camps cover a range of topics, including research methodology, data analysis, and academic writing. By participating in these events, our members are able to develop their research skills and contribute to the advancement of the industry

Leadership​

ASCM’s administrative council and advisory board, composed of academicians and industry leaders, oversee our operations and provide strategic direction to support our mission in developing the potential of students and young professionals in Bangladesh. Their diverse perspectives enable us to develop innovative solutions to the complex challenges, making sure that ASCM remains at the forefront of the industry.

ASCM Graduate Network​

ASCM Graduate Network (AGN) is a professional network of the alumni of Alliance for Supply Chain Management (ASCM), and serves as an ambassador for this organization. Through its various initiatives, AGN supports ASCM’s mission of promoting sustainable practices in supply chain management and business, and helps to connect and engage ASCM alumni to promote the next generation of supply chain professionals in Bangladesh.

Dr. Md. Mohibul Islam

Dr. Md. Mohibul Islam has completed his Ph.D. degree (2023) in the field of Supply Chain and Logistics Management from Nagoya Institute of Technology (NITech), Japan. He was a MEXT scholar from 2019-2023. He has been awarded the President Publication Award 2022 for his Ph.D. research publication from NITech, Japan. His research theme focuses on the development of mathematical models and algorithms to solve industrial problems, mainly related to the fields of Supply Chain and Logistics Management. He is currently working on developing various logistics model considering an unsteady demand and supply environment. He is also interested in developing different integrated multi-criteria group decision-making (MCGDM) models using Advanced Fuzzy Concept to solve decision-management problems in real-world industrial settings. He is the author of about 26 International Conference papers and journal articles. Besides, he is a member of the Japan Society of Mechanical Engineers (JSME) and the Institute of Engineers in Bangladesh (IEB). In the earlier, he has also completed his M. Sc. Engineering (2015) and B. Sc. Engineering (2010) degrees in the field of Industrial & Production Engineering (IPE) at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET), Bangladesh. He has been working as an Associate Professor in the Department of IPE at RUET since January 17, 2024.
